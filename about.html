About

Eric is originally from Kaysville, Utah, and has worked as a performer and educator throughout Northern Utah and Colorado. In addition to countless school ensembles he has played with the Utah Festival Opera orchestra, the American Festival Orchestra, the Greeley Philharmonic Orchestra, and the Orchestra at Temple Square. He has played extensively as a chamber musician, traveling across the country and performing in a variety of ensembles. As a member of the Bierstadt Brass Quintet he was a national finalist in the American Prize in Chamber Music Performance competition in 2018 and a winner of the Southard Music Competition in 2019, and has presented at trombone conferences in Colorado and Texas as part of the Very Good Trombone Quartet.
